Our School Facilities

Library

Our well-stocked library offers a vast collection of books, research materials, and digital resources for students.

Science Labs

We have state-of-the-art science labs equipped with modern tools to encourage hands-on learning.

Sports Complex

Our sports facilities include a gymnasium, football field, basketball court, and swimming pool to promote physical fitness.

Technology Center

The school boasts a fully equipped computer lab with high-speed internet and the latest software for student learning.

Auditorium

Our spacious auditorium is used for school events, drama performances, and guest lectures.
