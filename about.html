Our History

The Government has launched the Karnataka Residential Educational Institutional Society on 10.1999 with the objective of promoting the educational development of various classes of rural children in the state and to extend the scope of the same institution to cover all classes of schools. Morarji Desai / Smt. Indira Gandhi / Dr.B.R Ambedkar / Hutatma Residential Schools (MDRS / IGRS / DBRARS/NGRS/HRS) These residential schools also work in co-education from 6th to 10th grade. The total sanction number of students per school is 250, 50 students per class. 50 per cent of seats are provided for girls. Kittur Rani Chennamma Residential Schools (KRCRS) These residential schools are for girls from 6th to 10th grade in English medium only, The total sanction number of students per school is 250, 50 students per class Atal Bihari Vajpayee Residential Schools These residential schools also work in co-education from 6th to 12th grade. The total sanctioned number of students per class is 80 students (2 classes per 40 students). Ekalavya Model Residential SchoolsThese Residential Schools sanctioned by the Ministry of Tribal Affairs of the Central Government to provide quality education to the Scheduled Tribes students and are funded by the Central Government for the construction and construction of these residential schools. There are currently 12 boarding schools sanctioned
